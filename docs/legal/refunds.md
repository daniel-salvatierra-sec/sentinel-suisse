# Refund policy — LinkSwiss Premium (provisional)

**Status:** Draft until first live charge  
**Date:** 2026-07-20

## Scope

Applies to **LinkSwiss Premium** subscriptions paid via Stripe (card and TWINT when enabled).

## Cooling-off / refunds

1. You may request a refund within **14 days** of the first charge if you have not used Premium features beyond signup (no WhatsApp channel added, no more than the free trial alert).
2. After the cooling-off period, subscriptions renew until cancelled in Stripe Customer Portal / support.
3. Refunds are processed via Stripe to the original payment method (typically 5–10 business days).

## How to request

Email the privacy/contact address listed in the Terms with your account email and Stripe receipt.

## Chargebacks

Disputed payments may suspend Premium until resolved. Fraudulent chargebacks may lead to account closure.

This document is not legal advice; update with counsel before charging customers in Switzerland.
